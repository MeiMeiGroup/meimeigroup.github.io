Privacy Policy for SoundBoard

Last Updated: July 30, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how SoundBoard ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect the following types of information to improve your experience:

  • Usage Data (Anonymous): We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as the features you use, the sounds you play, and session duration. This data is aggregated and anonymized to help us understand user behavior and improve the App.
  • Device and Analytics Information: We may collect non-personally identifiable information about your device, such as its model, operating system version, and an anonymized advertising identifier (like Apple's IDFA). This helps us with analytics, compatibility, and serving relevant ads.
  • User-Generated Content: If you use the "Create My Sound" feature, your audio recordings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to these recordings.

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

iCloud Backup & Restore

The App offers an optional iCloud Backup & Restore feature. If you choose to use this feature, your app data, including your settings and any sounds you have recorded, will be uploaded and stored in your personal iCloud account, which is managed by Apple. We do not have access to the data stored in your iCloud account. Your use of iCloud is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and iCloud Terms and Conditions.

Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services for advertising and analytics. These services may collect their own data. We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob & Firebase Analytics
  • AppLovin
  • InMobi
  • Chartboost
  • Vungle
  • Start.io (formerly StartApp)

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
